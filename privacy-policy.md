# Privacy Policy

**LifeHabit**

**Last Updated:** April 2, 2026

## 1. Introduction

LifeHabit ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Habit Data:** Habit names, completion records, streaks, and notes you create within the app.
- **Preferences:** Language selection, notification preferences, and app settings.
- **Account Information:** If you sign in via Apple or Google, we receive your name and email as provided by the authentication provider.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for crash reporting and analytics.
- **Usage Data:** App interaction data such as feature usage frequency and session duration. This data is collected anonymously and used solely to improve the app experience.

### 2.3 AI Coach Feature
- If you use the AI Coach feature (premium), your habit data may be sent to third-party AI services (OpenAI) to generate personalized insights. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the App's core functionality.
- Generate habit statistics, streaks, and Discipline Score calculations.
- Send local notifications and reminders you have scheduled.
- Provide AI-powered insights and recommendations (premium feature).
- Improve app performance and user experience.
- Process subscriptions and purchases through Apple App Store.

## 4. Data Storage

- **Local Storage:** All habit data, preferences, and statistics are stored locally on your device using SQLite. We do not store your habit data on external servers.
- **Cloud Sync:** If you create an account, minimal authentication data is stored via Firebase Authentication. Your habit data remains on your device.
- **AI Requests:** AI Coach requests are processed in real-time and not stored by us after the response is delivered.

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase Authentication:** For anonymous and social sign-in. [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **RevenueCat:** For subscription management. [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
- **OpenAI:** For AI Coach feature. [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
- **Apple App Store:** For payment processing. [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy)

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes only.

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

- Your data is stored locally on your device for as long as you use the App.
- You can delete all your data at any time through Settings → Delete Account. This action is irreversible and removes all local data immediately.
- Uninstalling the App will also remove all locally stored data.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 9. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access all your data (available within the App's export feature).
- Delete all your data at any time.
- Opt out of AI Coach data processing by not using the feature.
- Modify your notification and data preferences at any time.

## 10. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. All data is stored locally with device-level encryption. AI Coach communications use HTTPS encryption.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
